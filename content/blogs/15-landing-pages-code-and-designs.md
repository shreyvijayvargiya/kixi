But one big question Jeol have is how to make high-converting landing pages

Key factors for high-converting landing pages

- Explains your idea within one page in a few words
- Propose the startup solution as a better alternative
- Call to action (Motivate visitors to sign up or test the product)
- Lays the feedback mechanism to know which problem to solve first and which one to push for later
- Faqa, pricing, subscribe form, calendar book meeting, payment page, etc

Each landing page deserves a bit of attention to make sure the problem and solution are well-explained
